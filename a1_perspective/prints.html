How Do Photo Prints Work?

In today's age of instantly posting photographs to social media outlets, the real visual beauty of stills can be lost. There is just something different about printing out one of your masterpieces, and then framing it on your wall, or putting it in a photo album, or sending a physical copy to family and friends.

Good news for you, if you really enjoy my work, you can buy your own prints of them! Not only are you receiving a tangible print that you can look at everyday, but you are supporting me as a photographer!

The most common sizes range from:

  • 4x6 inches
  • 5x7 inches
  • 8x12 inches
  • 12x18 inches
