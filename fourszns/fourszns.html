The Seasons

There are typically four distinct climate periods we experience on Earth: Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter (unless you live in Montana, then there are only three). According to timeanddate.com, these seasons occur because the "Earth's axis is slightly tilted in relation to its orbit around the Sun". The Four Seasons Make Music Too?

Did you hear the Four Seasons make music too? Well, not the literal seasons (Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter can't sing silly). But there is an American rock-n-roll group known as the "Four Seasons" that was pumping out iconic hits in the 1960s! Some of their best songs include Sherry, Big Girls Don't Cry, Walk Like A Man, and Oh, What a Night. With a cast of Frankie Vallie, Tommy DeVito, Bobby Gaudio, and Nick Massi, the Four Seasons left an indelible mark on the music industry and are still beloved today!
