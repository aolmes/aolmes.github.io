Winter

Winter time takes place during the months of December, January, and February. The temperature drops, often drastically. The snow and the rain come, with overcast days with little sun. The days begin to shorten, forcing people to hit the slopes and ice rinks, or stay inside drinking warm drinks and sharing hardy meals with family and friends.



"Winter is the time for comfort, for good food and warmth, for the touch of a friendly hand and for a talk beside the fire: it is the time for home."

-Edith Sitwell
