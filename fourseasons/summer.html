Summer

The summer months run from June, July, and August. Summer is arguable the best season of all. School is out, everyone is on vacation, and it is a time to relax and soak up some sun! Nothing beats the warm weather, sunny days, and beautiful sunests. People can finally take full advantage of destination spots such as beaches, lakes, and cities around the world!
