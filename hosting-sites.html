Taking a look at Web Hosting Providers: HostGator Cloud, Hostinger, and GoDaddy

HostGator Cloud: Most Unlimited Hosting

Pros

  • unlimited bandwidth and storage
  • fast load times (0.43ms)
  • free site migration if you switch web hosts

    • Cons

  • higher site renewal fees (goes from $2.99/mo to $14.95/mo after 6 months)

    • Click here to visit HostGator

    Hostinger:Lowest Price

    Pros

  • only $.80/mo
  • very fast load speeds (0.37ms)
  • 30 day money-back guarantee
  • databases in several regions

    • Cons

  • 99.91% uptime (could be better)
  • costs more money do add SSL certificate and priority support

    • Click here to visit Hostinger

    GoDaddy: Most Established

    Pros

  • household name
  • solid up time (99.97%)
  • 100gb of website storage

    • Cons

  • 0.52/ms load speed (could be better)
  • unsatisfactory customer and IT support
  • need to spend more money for options like unlimited emails and SSL certificate

    • Click here to visit GoDaddy

    My Pick

    After reviewing these three web-hosting platforms, I would choose HostGator Cloud. Although it is more expensive to use longterm, I provides me with unlimited bandwidth, unlimited storage, unlimited email accounts, and a 99.98% uptime. Not only that, but it comes with a SSL certificate, daily backups, and usefula and reliable live chats and support. If you want the best, you have to pay a little more to get the complete package.
